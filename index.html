Our Services

Extensive network with sake breweries

We at SAKE Street offer a broad range of quality sake labels made possible by our own extensive network with breweries throughout Japan which we have independently established through our sake specialty store operation and website content creation.

Quick quotation

We send within 3 business days of your contact a proposed list of export-ready sake labels with a quotation*.
*FCA prices. For FOB, CIF, or other incoterms, we will prepare them separately upon request.

Swift and consistent support

Customers may contact us with questions or inquiries about sake as well as about export transactions at their convenience. We respond promptly to inquiries via chat (WhatsApp or LINE). If necessary, we may set up an online meeting.

Import Procedure

1

Customer Data Entry

Enter customer data in the Quote Request Form.

2

Sake Labels Selection

We send you a list of export-ready sake labels with prices* based on your input data within 3 business days. Select sake labels of your interest and reply.
*FCA prices. If you require FOB or CIF prices, please indicate in the commentary field on Quote Request Form.

3

Order Finalization

Order items, terms of trade, export schedule, logistics, payment terms, and other details are finalized. Communication will take place via a channel of your choice including email, LINE, WhatsApp, among others.

4

Shipment of Invoice, Packing List and Other Necessary Documents

Based on your order, we will send you the invoice, packing list, and other documents necessary for the import (e.g., Certificate of Origin).

5

Shipment of Sake

We ship your order to the designated port.

About Us

SAKE Street is a sake venture business founded in 2018. Based in Tokyo, we operate a sake retail shop, handle sake export, and run a sake website (total 100k+ MAUs). Staff members as well as the founder Toshi Fujita have joined the industry with non-sake backgrounds, and are excited to be spreading sake love around the world.

Who We Are

Toshi Fujita

Toshi is first and foremost a sake aficionado. This has led him to found "SAKE Street" in 2018, where he will be able to distribute sake specially selected by him across the globe. Before SAKE Street, Toshi worked for notable financial institutions like HSBC and UBS in Tokyo and London.
